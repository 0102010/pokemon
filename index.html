History

The first episode of the Pokemon anime first aired on 1997 in Japan.
Pokemon are most impressive beings who have mysterious and wonderful powers and live in cooperation with humans.
It is a story of a boy named Ash Ketchum who wants to be the best in Pokemon battling or in his own words his goal is to be a Pokemon master.

Into the Pokemon World

The Pokemon world just like our world has different regions the diference is that the Pokemon world has 8 regions which are:

  1. Kanto
  2. Jhoto
  3. Hoenn
  4. Sinnoh
  5. Unova
  6. Kalos
  7. Alola
  8. Galar

In the Pokemon world people interact with Pokemon in different ways:

  • People who battle with Pokemon or take care of a Pokemon are called trainers.
  • People who work in Pokemon hosptials are called Pokemon doctors.

Evolution

Evolution is a phenomena which occurs between Pokemon when they want to become stronger when a Pokemon evolves it becomes Stronger also, once a Pokemon evolves it can not turn back to it's previous form although in Mega Evolution a Pokemon can return to it's previous form.

Gimmicks

Most of the Pokemon have 2 evoloutions(Excluding Mega Evolution) while some Pokemon have 1 and some have none.
There are some special things called "gimmicks" which can be used to raise the power of a Pokemon to it's limit and each of these gimmicks have roots in different regions 2 of the below mentioned gimmicks can be used in every 8 of the regions while 1 one of them can only be used in certain places and only in the Galar region, there are 3 types of gimmicks in the Pokemon anime.

  • Mega Evolution (can only occur using a mega stone and a key Stone)
  • Z-move (can only occur using a Z-Ring and a Z-Crystal)
  • Dynamax or Gigantamax (can only occur using Dynamax band)
  • Battle Bond (can only occur when a trainer and Pokemon can truly become one, and only one Pokemon has this gimmicks in the Pokemon anime, that Pokemon is Ash's Greninja)

More About the Regions

Each Region has it's own Pokemon league in which trainers can enter once they have earned 8 badges from 8 different gyms in their respective regions while only the Alola region is an exception because in Alola there aren't any Pokemon gym's or gym leaders they have Kahunas in the Alola region and Island trails which a trainers has to pass in order to battle in the Alola Pokemon League.
The Pokemon league in different regions allow the strongest trainers to have battles with each other until 1 trainer and their Pokemon win the whole league and then the winner trainer can take on the champion of the regions an dif the trainers wins then he/she can be the new champion of the region and if the trainer doesn't win then the champion remains the same.
While there are more than 800 Pokemon discovered as a whole in all of the regions each regions have it's special kind of Pokemon.
